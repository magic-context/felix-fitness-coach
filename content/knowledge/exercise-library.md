## Common Substitutions

| If You Can't Do | Try Instead | Why |
|-----------------|-------------|-----|
| Barbell Squat | Goblet Squat or Leg Press | Lower skill requirement, similar muscles |
| Pull-ups | Lat Pulldown or Band-Assisted | Build toward pull-ups progressively |
| Overhead Press | Landmine Press | Shoulder-friendly pressing angle |
| Barbell Bench | Dumbbell Bench or Push-ups | More shoulder freedom, lower barrier |
| Conventional Deadlift | Trap Bar Deadlift or RDL | More back-friendly hip hinge |
| Dips | Close-Grip Bench Press | Same muscles, less shoulder stress |
